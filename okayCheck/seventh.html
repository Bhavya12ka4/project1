About

Kumbhalgarh Fort, a formidable stronghold in Rajasthan, is often referred to as the "Great Wall of India." Built in the 15th century by Rana Kumbha, this majestic citadel boasts a 36-kilometer-long wall and stands as a testament to Rajput architecture and military might.
